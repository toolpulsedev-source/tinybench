Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Short version: tinybench.dev does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data. All tools run entirely in your browser. Your data never leaves your device.

Who we are

tinybench.dev is a free collection of developer tools operated as a static website. We do not have user accounts, a database, or a server that processes your data.

Information we do NOT collect

We do not collect:

  • Any data you paste or type into our tools
  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Passwords or authentication credentials
  • Files you upload (e.g. images for compression)
  • Payment information

All tool processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Nothing is sent to any server.

Cookies

tinybench.dev itself does not set any first-party cookies. However, third-party services we use (Google AdSense) may set cookies on your device. Please see our Cookie Policy for full details.

You can opt out of personalised advertising via Google's Ad Settings or by visiting aboutads.info.

Analytics

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to site owners. Cookies can be "session" cookies (deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent" cookies (which remain until they expire or you delete them).

First-party cookies

tinybench.dev does not set any first-party cookies. We do not use cookies to track users, store preferences, or maintain sessions.

How to manage cookies

You have several options for managing cookies:

  • Browser settings: You can configure your browser to refuse all or some cookies, or to alert you when websites set or access cookies. See your browser's help documentation for instructions.
  • Google opt-out: You can opt out of personalised Google advertising at google.com/settings/ads.
  • Industry opt-out: Visit aboutads.info/choices or youronlinechoices.eu (EU).

Please note that restricting cookies may impact the functionality of some websites you visit, though it will not affect your use of tinybench.dev's tools.
